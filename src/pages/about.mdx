## Who am I?

Michael has been developing software for over {yearsDeveloping} years for companies varying in size from small teams to enterprise.
He graduated from the Full Stack Web Developer program from [Nashville Software School](https://nashvillesoftwareschool.com) in 2017.
Before then, he was writing code just as a hobby to support his creative habits.
When Michael isn't in front of his computer, he can be found backpacking in the woods, dancing at music festivals, or eating at the new restaurant in town.
He lives in Nashville with his wife and pets.
